Automating Pathology Filing

Taunton Vale Healthcare, a busy Somerset practice serving over 14,000 patients, faced growing pressure to manage thousands of routine blood tests. Filing normal results was consuming valuable clinician time daily — time that should’ve been spent on patient care.

That’s where ApolloIQ stepped in.

‍

The Solution:

ApolloIQ deployed a pathology automation system capable of automatically filing 70–80% of blood test results that fell within normal ranges.

This included tests like:

  • Full Blood Count (FBC)
  • Liver Function Tests (LFTs)
  • Urea & Electrolytes (U&Es)
  • Lipid Profiles, Inflammatory Markers
  • CKD indicators, Vitamin D, B12, Folate

With predefined clinical thresholds in place, the system applied intelligent actions like File & Comment, File No Comment, or Send Task — allowing clinicians to focus only on the results that mattered most.

‍

Results at a Glance:

  • 30,234 total tests processed in 3 months
  • 11,252 filed automatically, saving significant clinician time
  • £4,901 in savings over 3 months → projected £19,604 annually
  • 100% accuracy rate in automated filing
  • Continuous safety oversight by a Clinical Safety Officer
