Forestside Medical Practice - Clinical Document management automation

Forestside Medical Practice serves 11,000 patients and manages thousands of clinical documents every month. But before automation, two admin staff were manually coding and filing until 4 PM every day — often overwhelmed, prone to delays, and unable to focus on value-based tasks.

ApolloIQ introduced an automated document management solution using Robotic Process Automation (RPA) and a self-hosted AI language model, integrated seamlessly with EMIS Web.

THE CHALLENGE

100 Letters a Day. 36,500 a Year. All Processed Manually.

With 11,000 registered patients, Forestside received around 100 clinical letters per day — referral letters, hospital discharge summaries, specialist clinic letters, 111 reports, A&E reports, and investigation results. Each document required manual coding, labelling, dating, and filing — a time-consuming process prone to human error and delays.

The Solution

ApolloIQ deployed an intelligent system combining Robotic Process Automation (RPA) with a self-hosted AI language model to automate and enhance the processing of clinical documents at Forestside.

  • Accurate Date Identification — RPA extracts the correct letter date from each document.
  • SnomedCT Coding — Automatically matches document types with clinical codes.
  • AI-Generated Titles — Self-hosted language model creates precise, meaningful document titles.
  • Secure & Private — Operates within the HSCN connection. Patient data never leaves the network.
  • Available 24/7-365 — Processes documents around the clock with no breaks needed.
THE RESULT

Every Document Coded, Titled, and Filed Automatically

After ApolloIQ was deployed at Forestside, every document received the correct date, a meaningful AI-generated title, and a proper SnomedCT code — ready for clinical review.
