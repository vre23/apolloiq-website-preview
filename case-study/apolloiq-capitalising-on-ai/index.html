Forestside Medical Practice - Clinical Document Management Automation

Forestside Medical Practice serves 11,000 patients and receives around 100 clinical letters per day. Before ApolloIQ, two admin staff were manually coding and filing documents until 4 PM every day -- prone to delays and human error.

ApolloIQ deployed an intelligent automation system combining RPA and a self-hosted AI language model, integrated with EMIS Web -- transforming how Forestside processes clinical documents.

The Solution

ApolloIQ deployed a clinical document management system that automatically processes every incoming letter:

  • Accurate Date Identification -- RPA extracts the correct letter date
  • SnomedCT Coding -- Matches document types with clinical codes
  • AI-Generated Titles -- Precise, meaningful document titles
  • Secure & Private -- Operates within the HSCN connection
  • Available 24/7-365 -- No breaks, no holidays needed
