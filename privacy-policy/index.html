Our Privacy Policy

At ApolloIQ, we are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This privacy notice explains how we collect, use, store, and disclose your personal information. It also outlines your rights and choices regarding your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following types of personal information:

  • Contact information (such as name, email address, phone number)
  • Demographic information (such as location, preferences)
  • Billing information
  • Other information necessary to provide our services

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide our services and fulfill our contractual obligations
  • To communicate with you and respond to your inquiries
  • To improve our services and enhance your user experience
  • To send you promotional materials and updates (with your consent, where required by law)

3. Information Sharing

We may share your personal information with the following parties:

  • Our trusted service providers and business partners who assist us in delivering our services
  • Regulatory authorities or legal entities as required by law or to protect our rights

4. Data Security

We have implemented appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure the security of your personal information and protect it against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse.

5. Data Retention

We will retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy notice, or as required by applicable laws and regulations.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and request a copy of your personal information
  • Update or correct your personal information
  • Request the erasure of your personal information
  • Object to or restrict the processing of your personal information
  • Withdraw your consent for any processing activities that rely on consent

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding your personal information or this privacy notice, please contact us using the following details:

Email: contact@apolloiq.co.uk

We may update this privacy notice from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We encourage you to review this notice periodically for any updates.

If you are not satisfied with our response to your privacy-related concerns, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the relevant supervisory authority.

Thank you for entrusting us with your personal information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in our data practices.
