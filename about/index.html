Who we are

Why ApolloIQ?

Founded in Taunton, Somerset, our team is a unique blend of dedicated Clinical and Software Engineers, all working together to create innovative solutions that address the challenges faced by healthcare providers today.

  • Clinical Expertise: Our team has extensive clinical experience spanning hundreds of GP surgeries and pharmacies.
  • Proven Results: We deliver solutions that significantly reduce administrative burdens and operational costs.
  • Endorsement and Trust: Fully endorsed by Somerset ICB, our products are trusted by healthcare providers across the region.
Find out more
OUR STORY

Born out of a deep understanding of the healthcare landscape, ApolloIQ was established by a team of committed clinicians who experienced firsthand the high administrative burden in GP surgeries and pharmacies across the South West. Our strong ties with local Primary Care Networks (PCNs) and Integrated Care Board (ICB) ensure that our solutions are tailored to the real-world needs of healthcare providers.

OUR MISSION

We believe that every healthcare provider should have access to advanced automation tools that can streamline their workflows, reduce their operational costs, and ultimately improve patient outcomes.

OUR TEAM

Our team is made up of experts from various fields, including healthcare, technology, AI, and automation. Together, we bring decades of experience to the table, helping us craft innovative solutions that address the complexities of modern healthcare.

OUR VALUES

Innovation: We continually push the boundaries of what's possible with AI and automation, staying at the forefront of healthcare technology.

Efficiency: Our solutions are designed to simplify and streamline workflows, allowing healthcare providers to focus on delivering exceptional patient care.

Accuracy: We ensure 100% precision in clinical tasks, reducing human error and enhancing the reliability of medical processes.

Collaboration: We work closely with our clients to design bespoke solutions that meet their unique needs and challenges.

Patient-Centered: At the heart of our technology is the goal to improve patient outcomes, making healthcare faster, more accessible, and more reliable.

OUR JOURNEY

Founded in 2023, ApolloIQ was built on the belief that technology should serve healthcare, not complicate it. Over the years, we have grown from a small start-up to a trusted partner for healthcare practices and networks across the UK. We are proud to continue pushing the boundaries of what AI and automation can achieve in the healthcare industry.
