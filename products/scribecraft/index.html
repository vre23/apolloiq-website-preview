OVERVIEW

What is ScribeCraft?

ScribeCraft is an AI-powered clinical documentation tool that listens to clinician-patient consultations, transcribes the conversation in real time, and automatically generates structured, accurate clinical notes — ready to copy into your EPR system.

Up to 70% of consultation time is lost to data entry. The leading cause of clinician burnout is after-hours administrative work. ScribeCraft eliminates this burden by handling the documentation so you can focus on what matters: the patient.

ScribeCraft is a registered Class I medical device, designed as an administrative support tool. It does not provide diagnoses, suggest treatments, or replace clinical judgement. Every note generated is a draft that requires clinician review and validation before use.

Key Features

Purpose-built for NHS and UK primary care. ScribeCraft understands medical terminology and structures notes the way clinicians need them.

  • Live Consultation Capture
    Transcribes and structures your patient conversations in real time — face-to-face, telephone, or video consultations.
  • Flexible Clinical Formats
    Generates notes in SOAP format, clinical letters, referral letters, and other structured templates to match your workflow.
  • Multi-Specialty Support
    Trained for General Practice, Cardiology, Paediatrics, Neurology, Dermatology, Mental Health, and many more specialties.
  • EPR-Ready Output
    Clean, structured text ready for one-click copy-paste into EMIS Web, SystmOne, Vision, or any EPR system. Also exportable as PDF.
  • Teleconsultation Ready
    Works seamlessly with video and telephone consultations — simply run ScribeCraft on the same device as your call.
  • No Installation Required
    ScribeCraft is a web application that runs directly in your browser — Chrome, Edge, Firefox, or Safari. Works on desktop, laptop, tablet, or phone.

How Does It Work?

ScribeCraft is built for clinical ease — no steep learning curve, no interruptions, just seamless transcription. Set up takes 15 minutes and you start saving time from the very first consultation.

  • 1. Log into Merlin
    Access ScribeCraft through your dedicated account on the Merlin platform. No software to install — it runs in your browser.
  • 2. Choose a Note Format
    Select from the available clinical templates — SOAP notes, clinical letters, referral letters, or specialty-specific formats.
  • 3. Start a New Session
    Press "New Session" to begin recording. ScribeCraft is ready to capture your consultation with the patient.
  • 4. Speak Naturally
    Conduct your consultation as usual. ScribeCraft records and transcribes in real time using advanced speech recognition optimised for medical terminology.
  • 5. Notes Are Structured Automatically
    When you stop the session, the transcription is analysed and organised into your chosen template — a complete, structured clinical note ready for review.
  • 6. Review, Copy, and File
    Read and validate the draft, then copy it directly into your EPR system or export it as a PDF for printing or patient records.
SAFETY & SECURITY

Built for Clinical Trust

ScribeCraft is designed from the ground up to meet the highest standards of data protection and clinical safety required by UK healthcare.
  • Clinician in Control (Human-in-the-Loop)
    ScribeCraft is an administrative support tool — it does not make clinical decisions. Every document generated is a draft that must be reviewed and validated by the clinician before use. The clinician retains full responsibility for the final content.
  • Zero-Retention Architecture
    Audio data is processed in real time and immediately deleted. No recordings or patient data are retained on our servers. Zero-retention by design.
  • UK GDPR & Data Protection Act 2018 Compliant
    All data processing complies with UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018. Your practice is the Data Controller; ApolloIQ is the Data Processor, formalised through a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) signed at account activation.
  • Encryption at Every Layer
    All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256). Only the authenticated user can access their generated documents — the ApolloIQ team has no access to document content.
  • Data Residency
    All data is stored and processed exclusively within the UK and European Economic Area (Microsoft Azure). Patient data never leaves these regions.
  • AI Training Guarantee
    Your data is never used to train, retrain, or improve any AI models. This is contractually guaranteed in our Terms of Service and DPA.
PATIENT CONSENT

Transparent and Compliant

Before using ScribeCraft in a consultation, the patient must be informed and their consent obtained. This is a legal requirement under UK data protection law and good clinical practice.
  • What to tell the patient: That the consultation will be recorded by an AI tool to generate clinical documentation, how the data is processed and stored, and that they can decline without any impact on their care.
  • Written consent: ApolloIQ provides a customisable patient consent form template compliant with UK GDPR.
  • Verbal consent: Acceptable if documented in the patient record. Suggested phrasing: "I'll be using a digital assistant that transcribes our conversation to generate clinical notes. Are you happy with that?"
  • If the patient declines: The consultation proceeds normally without ScribeCraft. The patient's refusal has no effect on the quality of care received.
We recommend displaying an informational poster in your waiting room. ApolloIQ provides printable patient-facing materials free of charge.

Compliance & Certifications

ScribeCraft meets the regulatory requirements for use in UK healthcare settings.

  • Class I Medical Device

  • UK GDPR Compliant
    Fully compliant with UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018, processing sensitive health data (Article 9 equivalent) to the highest standards.
  • ISO 27001 Standards
    Information security management aligned with ISO 27001 standards, ensuring robust protection of clinical data.
  • AI Act Ready
    Designed with full transparency obligations in accordance with the EU AI Act (Regulation 2024/1689). All AI-generated content is clearly identified as requiring human validation.
  • Microsoft for Startups Partner
    Built on Microsoft Azure infrastructure, benefiting from enterprise-grade security, redundancy, and compliance frameworks.
