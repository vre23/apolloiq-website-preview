THE PROBLEM

Medication Shortages Are a Clinical Risk

In the UK, medication shortages are not just an administrative hurdle — they are a clinical risk. When a medication is out of stock, the traditional "manual chase" takes an average of 10 minutes per item, as pharmacists ring around suppliers trying to source alternatives. This has significant downstream effects on the patient:

  • Treatment Delays: Patients often wait 24–48 hours longer for essential medication while pharmacists "ring around."
  • Reduced Adherence: Complexity in obtaining medication leads to missed doses and therapeutic failure.
  • Patient Anxiety: Patients experience increased stress when their care team cannot provide immediate answers regarding supply.
  • Safety Risks: Manual workarounds or switching to less familiar alternatives due to perceived unavailability can lead to prescribing errors.
THE SOLUTION

What is Locate Elixir?

Locate Elixir digitises the procurement and sourcing workflow. By providing real-time national availability and sourcing data in 10 seconds, it allows pharmacists to resolve a supply issue during the clinical review or while the patient is still in contact — ensuring continuity of care and immediate peace of mind.

The tool queries a database updated every few minutes from a medication broker partner and returns a clear traffic light status for any medication searched.

Traffic Light System

Instantly understand medication availability at a glance with three clear statuses.

In Stock Orders have been successfully placed for this product recently. It is very likely to be available from suppliers at a national level.
Out of Stock An order has been placed for this product, but it was out of stock at the time of the order.
No Record No orders have been put through the system for this product. There is no reliable information about its availability. This does not mean "Out of Stock."

How Does It Work?

Locate Elixir is intuitive and fast — designed for clinical environments with no learning curve.

  1. Log into Merlin Access Locate Elixir through your dedicated account on the Merlin platform. No software to install — it runs in your browser.
  2. Select Locate Elixir From the left-hand menu, select the "Spells" tab and click on "Locate Elixir" to open the search tool.
  3. Search for a Medication Type the name of the drug you wish to check into the search bar — our smart search helps you find it instantly.
  4. Interpret the Traffic Light Result The tool returns one of three statuses: In Stock (green), Out of Stock (red), or No Record (grey). Use this data to prescribe confidently.

Safety & Limitations

Important information to ensure safe and effective use of Locate Elixir.

National Availability Only Locate Elixir displays national availability, not local pharmacy or hospital stock. A status of "In Stock" does not guarantee the medication is available at your specific location.
User Verification Required While we strive to ensure information is accurate and timely, availability may change rapidly. It is the responsibility of the user to verify critical availability directly with the pharmacy or supplier before acting.
"No Record" Is Not "Out of Stock" The "No Record" status means the availability is unknown — no orders have been placed through the system. Do not use this status as the sole basis for a clinical decision.
Clinical Judgement This tool is for information and decision support only. It does not replace your professional clinical judgement.
