Spells

Scribe Craft

ScribeCraft is a voice-powered consultation tool that listens to clinician-patient interactions and instantly creates structured, accurate notes. Designed to integrate smoothly within the Merlin workspace, it allows clinicians to quickly copy the output into their EPR system — all without typing a word.

Blood Pressure Scroll

Blood Pressure Scroll is a powerful AI-driven tool designed to support GP practices in digitising blood pressure records submitted by patients. Often, these readings come in handwritten formats, making manual transcription time-consuming and prone to error.

Locate Elixir

Locate Elixir is a live medication availability tool designed to give healthcare staff real-time visibility of pharmacy stock levels. Using a simple traffic light system, users can instantly check whether a medication is available to order (green) or currently out of stock (red). This reduces prescribing delays.

Automated Workflows

Pathology Automation

Automate up to 80% of blood test filings, saving approximately £18,000 annually. Ensure 100% accuracy with automated filing for tests like Full Blood Count (FBC), Liver Function Tests (LFTs), and more.

Clinical Document Management

Document Management processes and organizes administrative paperwork, such as referrals and patient forms, directly within Merlin's workspace, significantly speeding up a task typically handled manually by admin staff.

Clinical Calculator (QRisk3)

Say goodbye to manual calculations. Our QRisk3 Calculations delivers accurate cardiovascular risk assessments instantly. Results can be uploaded in EPR.

GP List Reallocation

GP List Reallocation is a smart, automated tool designed to redistribute patients when a GP leaves a practice. Whether it's the entire patient list or just a subset, the system uses intelligent algorithms to assign patients fairly and strategically across your remaining clinicians.
